How Does Vermicompost Aid the Use of Chemical Fertilizers?

Chemical fertilizers have been a staple with conventional farmers across India and the world since the industrial revolution. However, it has been observed that chemical fertilizers may tend to damage soil quality and hamper plant quality in the long run. This is where vermicompost fertilizers come to the rescue for every type of farm land across India. Vermicompost fertilizers can be used to support the use of chemical fertilizers or even as an alternative to it to ensure that the natural fertility of the soil is maintained over the years.
